Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office, sending an email, or filling out the contact form on this page. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

What should I expect during my first wellness session?

Your first session will include a comprehensive assessment of your current health status, wellness goals, and lifestyle factors. We'll discuss your medical history, dietary habits, physical activity, stress levels, and sleep patterns to create a personalized wellness plan.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via secure video conferencing platforms. This option is available for initial consultations, follow-up appointments, and certain wellness coaching sessions.

How much do your wellness programs cost?

Our program costs vary depending on the type and duration of services. We offer different packages to accommodate various budgets and needs. Please contact us for detailed pricing information and available payment plans.
